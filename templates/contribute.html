How to Contribute

Contribute Code

First and foremost, Rotting Research is an ope-source project. It was build and continues to be improved by the community. Any documentation or code that you can contribute is appriciated and will help keep our project growing, as well as stable. We hope to continue to add features and technologies for years to come. Check out our projects page to view our repositories and learn how you can help.

Contribute Ideas

We hope to keep Rotting Research relavant and cutting-edge. This means new ideas, new features, and new research. Please feel to submit your ideas or feedback using this form. https://github.com/rottingresearch/rottingresearch/issues/new/choose .

Contribute Financially

Like any project, we do have expenses. There are regular expenses like hosting fees, Redis subscription, Cloudflare for security, and many others. We are members of the Open Source Collective, which allows us to accept donations, while remaining fully-transparent regarding our financials. Feel free to make a one-time donation or a reocurring one. Every bit helps keep us running. To make a donation, take a look at our funding page at https://opencollective.com/rottingresearch .
